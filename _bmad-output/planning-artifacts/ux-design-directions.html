Facility Manager

📍 Brussels ⏰ Full-time 🏢 ACEG 📅 Published 3 weeks ago

About the Role

We're looking for an experienced Facility Manager to oversee our Brussels headquarters and two satellite offices. You'll lead a team of 8 technicians and coordinate with external vendors to ensure our facilities run smoothly, safely, and sustainably.

What You'll Do

  • Manage day-to-day facility operations across 3 locations
  • Lead and develop a team of 8 facility technicians
  • Negotiate and manage vendor contracts (cleaning, security, HVAC)
  • Drive energy efficiency initiatives (target: 15% reduction in 2 years)
  • Ensure compliance with Belgian building regulations and safety standards

Requirements

  • ⚠️ Required: Valid driver's license (B)
  • ⚠️ Required: Dutch speaking (C1 or native)
  • ⭐ Preferred: 5+ years facility management experience
  • ⭐ Preferred: Team leadership experience
  • ⭐ Preferred: Knowledge of HVAC and building systems

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary with benefits package
  • Company car + fuel card
  • Meal vouchers and group insurance
  • Flexible working hours
  • Professional development budget
